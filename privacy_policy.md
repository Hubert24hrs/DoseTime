# Privacy Policy for DoseAlert

**Last updated: February 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how DoseAlert ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We DO NOT Collect
We do not collect or store your personal health data, medication schedules, or contact information on our servers. All sensitive data entered into the App is stored locally on your device using secure storage and SQLite databases.

## Information Automatically Collected
We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### 1. Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use:
*   Advertising ID (AAID)
*   Coarse Location data
*   Performance data
*   Device information
This data is used to provide personalized ads and analytics.
*   [Link to AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

### 2. Firebase (Google Analytics & Crashlytics)
We use Firebase to improve app stability and understand usage patterns.
*   **Crashlytics**: Collects crash logs and device state information when the app crashes.
*   **Analytics**: Collects anonymous usage data (e.g., screens visited, buttons clicked).
*   [Link to Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 3. RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage unauthorized in-app purchase subscriptions ("Pro" features). RevenueCat may collect:
*   App Store purchase history
*   Anonymous User IDs
*   [Link to RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## How We Use Your Information
*   **To Provide Services**: To manage your medication reminders locally.
*   **To Improve the App**: Using anonymous crash reports to fix bugs.
*   **To Monetize**: Displaying ads to free users to support development.

## Data Retention
*   **Local Data**: Retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
*   **Third-Party Data**: Retained according to the policies of the third-party providers listed above.

## Children's Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
