# Privacy Policy for DoseTime

**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

DoseTime ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Medication Information**: Names, dosages, and schedule times of medications you add
- **Dose Logs**: Records of when you take or skip medications
- **App Preferences**: Your notification settings and display preferences

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Analytics Data**: Anonymous app usage statistics (screens viewed, features used)
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information when the app crashes (device model, OS version, error logs)

## How Your Data is Stored

**All medication data is stored locally on your device.** We do not transmit your medication information to any external servers.

- Data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- Encryption keys are stored in secure device storage
- No cloud backup of medication data occurs

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google)
- Collects anonymous usage analytics
- Reports app crashes for improvement
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### RevenueCat (if Pro features purchased)
- Manages in-app purchase transactions
- Stores purchase receipts
- [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide medication reminder functionality
- Improve app performance and stability
- Develop new features based on usage patterns
- Process in-app purchases

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell your personal information.**

We may share data only:
- With service providers (Firebase, RevenueCat) as described above
- If required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or property

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data via the app's export feature
- **Delete** all your data by uninstalling the app or using the "Delete All Data" option
- **Opt-out** of analytics by disabling in app settings (coming soon)

## Data Retention

- Medication data: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
- Analytics data: Retained by Firebase for 14 months
- Crash reports: Retained by Firebase for 90 days

## Children's Privacy

DoseTime is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
- Secure local storage using Android Keystore
- No transmission of medication data over networks

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
